## II. Framing: The Necessary Evil of Cognition

To survive, the brain must lie.

**Obsession** thus arises:

We worship conceptual statues, missing the breathing warmth of the statues themselves.

"Understanding all principles but living an unsatisfactory life"—  
The principles aren't wrong; we **frame principles into dogma**, treating the **map as territory**.
